About Me

I am a Systems Engineer with a strong background in software development who, driven by a genuine passion for data, has transitioned my career toward the field of Data Science. This shift has not only been a natural evolution of my technical journey but also a conscious decision to explore how data can generate meaningful impact across organizations.


To support this transition, I have earned several industry-recognized certifications, including Certified Big Data Engineer and, more recently, Data Analyst. Through this learning path, I have developed strong skills in building data pipelines, data transformation, exploratory data analysis, modeling, and data visualization—all with a clear focus on driving value from information.


My goal is to continue growing in the field of Data Science, applying my knowledge and skills to solve complex problems and contribute to the success of organizations through data-driven decisions. I am excited about the opportunities that this field offers and I am committed to continue learning and adapting to new technologies and trends.
